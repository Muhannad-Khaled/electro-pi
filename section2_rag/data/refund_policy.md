# Wasl Eats — Refund Policy

Last updated: March 2026. This document explains when and how customers receive
refunds for orders placed through the Wasl Eats app. Refund handling depends on
the payment method used and the stage the order was in when the issue occurred.

## Cancellation Window

Customers can cancel an order for a full refund within **5 minutes** of placing
it, provided the restaurant has not yet accepted the order. Once the restaurant
marks the order as "Accepted" in its dashboard, the 5-minute window no longer
applies and cancellation is treated as a late cancellation.

Late cancellations (after acceptance but before the courier picks up the food)
are refunded at **50%** of the order subtotal. The delivery fee and service fee
are non-refundable in this case, because the courier has already been dispatched
or assigned.

Cancellations after courier pickup are not eligible for a refund. In this case
the customer should contact support to report the issue rather than cancelling.

## Missing or Incorrect Items

If part of the order is missing or incorrect, the customer is refunded only for
the affected items, not the full order. The item's price plus its proportional
share of the service fee is refunded. Delivery fee is not refunded for partial
issues.

If **more than 50%** of the order value is missing or incorrect, the full order
including delivery fee is refunded, and the customer is offered a 15% credit on
their next order as a goodwill gesture.

## Refund Processing Time

Refund processing time depends on the original payment method:

- **Credit or debit card:** 5–7 business days to appear on the statement.
- **Wasl Wallet:** instant (within 60 seconds of approval).
- **Cash on delivery:** refunded as Wasl Wallet credit only. Cash refunds are
  not issued directly to the customer, because no card transaction exists to
  reverse.
- **Apple Pay / Google Pay:** treated the same as the underlying card — 5–7
  business days.

If a refund does not appear within the stated window, the customer should
first check with their bank before contacting support, since interbank delays
are the most common cause.

## Non-Refundable Cases

The following are not eligible for a refund under any circumstances:

- Complaints about taste, portion size (when the item matches the menu
  description), or personal preference.
- Orders where the customer provided an incorrect or unreachable address and
  the courier waited more than 10 minutes at the given location.
- Promo code discounts. If an order is refunded, the promo code's monetary
  value is not returned separately — the refund is calculated on the amount
  actually charged.

## Escalation

If a customer disagrees with a refund decision, they can request escalation to
a human support agent through the in-app chat. Escalated cases are reviewed
within 24 hours on business days and within 48 hours on weekends and public
holidays.
