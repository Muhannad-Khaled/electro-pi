# Wasl Eats — Account and Order Management

This document covers what customers can and cannot change after placing an
order, how account data is handled, and the rules for account deletion.

## Order Modifications

Once an order is placed, modifications are limited by the order stage:

- **Within the first 3 minutes** and before restaurant acceptance: customers
  can add or remove items freely. The order total is recalculated and the
  payment method is re-authorized for the new amount.
- **After restaurant acceptance:** items can no longer be added or removed,
  because the restaurant may have already started preparation. Special
  instructions (for example, "no onions") can still be updated up to the
  "Preparing" stage, but the restaurant is not obligated to honor them at
  that point.
- **After the "Preparing" stage:** no modifications of any kind are allowed.

Delivery address changes follow different rules from item changes. See below.

## Changing the Delivery Address

The delivery address can be changed only if the new address is within **500
meters** of the original address. This restriction exists because a larger
change would affect the delivery fee, courier assignment, and possibly the
courier's willingness to accept the order.

Address changes are allowed up to the "On the way" stage. Once the courier is
en route, address changes require calling the courier directly through the
in-app call button and are at the courier's discretion.

If a customer needs to change to a fully different address, the correct
action is to cancel (subject to the refund policy's cancellation rules) and
place a new order.

## Order History and Retention

Order history is retained on the account for **24 months**. During this period,
customers can view full order details, download receipts, and re-order any
past order with a single tap. After 24 months, orders are archived: the
customer can still see the date and total, but individual items and receipts
are no longer accessible through the app.

Archived order data is retained by Wasl Eats for an additional 5 years for
accounting and legal compliance, then permanently deleted.

## Account Deletion

Customers can request account deletion from the app under Settings → Privacy
→ Delete Account. Deletion is not immediate:

- The account is **deactivated within 24 hours**, meaning it can no longer be
  used to place orders or log in.
- Personal data is **permanently deleted within 30 days**, except for records
  legally required to be retained (financial records, dispute history).
- Wasl Wallet balance is forfeited on deletion and cannot be refunded to a
  card. Customers with wallet balance should spend it down or transfer it
  before requesting deletion.

Deletion cannot be undone. A new account with the same email address can be
created after 30 days, but it will have no history, saved addresses, or
loyalty status from the deleted account.

## Notifications

Order-related notifications (order accepted, courier en route, delivered) are
enabled by default and can be disabled per-channel in Settings → Notifications.
Promotional notifications are opt-in only and are never enabled by default,
in line with local privacy regulations.

Customers who disable all order notifications are still shown in-app updates
on the tracking screen but will not receive push notifications, SMS, or
email for order events.
